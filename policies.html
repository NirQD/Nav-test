Our Core Policies

1. Quality Policy
We are dedicated to delivering premium-quality cotton compact yarn that meets global standards of consistency, strength, and performance.
Our commitment includes:
Maintaining strict quality control at every stage of production
Using internationally certified testing systems and advanced machinery
Continuous improvement through training, innovation, and upgraded technology
Ensuring customer satisfaction through reliable service and ethical practices
We strive to exceed expectations and remain a trusted partner in the textile industry.
2. Environmental & Sustainability Policy
We are committed to responsible manufacturing that respects our environment and future generations.
Our sustainability principles include:
Reducing carbon footprint through energy-efficient operations and clean energy
Using our 10.05 MW captive solar plant to meet a major share of our power needs
Minimizing waste through recycling, optimized material usage, and proper disposal
Conserving resources through responsible water and air management
Supporting global sustainability standards and certifications
At NAV Hindustan Spinners, sustainability is not an initiative — it is a continuous responsibility.
3. Health, Safety & Workplace Policy
We believe that a safe and healthy work environment is essential for productivity and employee well-being.
Our practices include:
Adhering strictly to industrial safety standards and government regulations
Ensuring safe operations through regular audits, monitoring, and preventive maintenance
Providing safety training, protective equipment, and emergency response systems
Promoting a culture of respect, teamwork, and transparency
Preventing discrimination and ensuring equal opportunities for all employees
Our people are our greatest strength, and their safety is our top priority.
4. Ethical Business & Compliance Policy
We operate with integrity and expect the same from all our partners and stakeholders.
Our principles include:
Zero tolerance for corruption, bribery, or unethical practices
Compliance with all applicable laws, regulations, and industry standards
Transparent communication and honest business dealings
Respecting confidential information and protecting customer data
Ethical sourcing and responsible supply chain management
We ensure every decision aligns with our values and long-term vision.
5. Privacy & Data Protection Policy
We respect your privacy and safeguard all information shared with us.
Our commitments include:
Collecting only the information necessary for communication or service
Keeping personal and business data secure and confidential
Never sharing or selling customer information to third parties
Protecting data through secure systems and authorized access
Allowing users to request updates or deletion of their shared data
Your trust is important to us, and we take data protection seriously.
6. Customer Support & Grievance Policy
We strive to provide a smooth and reliable experience for all our customers and partners.
Our approach includes:
Clear communication during enquiries, orders, and support
Timely resolution of concerns and operational issues
Dedicated contact channels for feedback and assistance
Continuous improvements based on customer suggestions
For support, you may contact our team directly through the information provided on the Contact Us page.
7. Policy Updates
NAV Hindustan Spinners (P) Ltd. reserves the right to update or modify these policies as required. Any changes will be reflected on this page to ensure transparency and compliance with updated standards.
Delivering excellence with reliability and trust.
