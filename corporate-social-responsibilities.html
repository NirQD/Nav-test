Empowering Education Through Support
Periyanaickenpalayam, June 24 / 2004 :
A notebook distribution program was held on Tuesday at the Government Primary School, Narasimhanaickenpalayam, organized by the Hindustan Spinners Trust.
The event was presided over by Panchayat President Padmalaya Srinivasan, with former President Narasimmalu Naidu in attendance. Headmaster M. Thannasi delivered the welcome address.
Trust Directors D. Selvaraj, Kamalam Duraisamy, and D. Nataraj distributed notebooks worth ₹20,000 to 275 students.
SSA Block Resource Centre Officer Velusamy, along with Pathe Ahamad and Arumugam, delivered felicitation speeches. Councillor Ganesan distributed pens to all students, and councillors Rangammal, Deivanai, Shanmugam, and Boopathy also participated.
The vote of thanks was delivered by Parents’ Association President Ahamad.
Educational Infrastructure Support – 2020
As part of our commitment to rural development, Nav Hindustan Spinners supported the educational growth of Rawthkollanur by contributing to the construction and improvement of school infrastructure.
Our initiatives included:
• Construction support for school facilities
• Installation of blackboards to enhance classroom learning
• Strengthening foundational educational resources for students
We believe that investing in education today builds stronger generations tomorrow.
Vinayagar Temple Construction – Kural Nagar, 2025
In 2025, Nav Hindustan Spinners contributed to the construction of a Vinayagar Temple at Kural Nagar, supporting the cultural and spiritual needs of the local community.
This initiative reflects our dedication to:
• Preserving cultural heritage
• Strengthening community bonds
• Creating shared spaces for unity and devotion
We believe development must nurture both progress and tradition.
