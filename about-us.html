About

Established in 1973 in Coimbatore’s vibrant textile belt, NAV Hindustan Spinners (P) Ltd. has built its reputation on trust, craftsmanship, and continuous innovation. Supported by 66,000+ spindles and a dedicated team of over 1,000 experts, the mill brings together heritage knowledge and modern engineering to create high-quality 100% cotton compact yarn for customers across India and worldwide.

1973 — The Beginning

A vision takes shape in Coimbatore’s textile hub, as NAV Hindustan Spinners is founded with a commitment to quality-driven yarn manufacturing.

1980s — Expanding Our Strength

Spindle capacity grows steadily, strengthening our ability to meet India’s rising demand for premium cotton yarn.

1990s — The Quality Transformation

Modern machinery and refined processes usher in a new era of consistency, precision, and superior yarn performance.

2000 — Entering Global Markets

Our yarn reaches international manufacturers, marking NAV Hindustan Spinners’ arrival on the global textile map with trusted reliability.

2010 — Technology-Led Modernization

A major upgrade phase introduces advanced spinning systems, automation, and precision engineering, elevating product quality and mill efficiency.

2020 — Sustainability Becomes Core

We adopt eco-conscious operations, energy-efficient systems, and responsible manufacturing practices, aligning with global sustainability standards.

2023 — 50 Years of Excellence

A proud milestone as we celebrate five decades of innovation, discipline, and service to the textile industry.

Today — Future

Ready Manufacturing with 66,000+ spindles, 1,000+ skilled professionals, and state-of-the-art technology, NAV Hindustan Spinners continues to craft premium yarn for domestic and global markets.
